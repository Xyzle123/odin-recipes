Sinigang Recipe


Sinigang is a soup that has a distinct sour and savoury taste

Ingredients

  • Pork Belly
  • A Bunch of Spinach
  • Fish Sauce
  • String Beans
  • Tomatoes
  • Chili
  • Cooking Oil
  • Water
  • Onion
  • Taro
  • Sinigang Mix

Steps

  1. Heat the pot and put-in the cooking oil
  2. Saut√© the onion until its layers separate from each other
  3. Add the pork belly and cook until outer part turns light brown
  4. Put-in the fish sauce and mix with the ingredients
  5. Pour the water and bring to a boil
  6. Add the flour gradually while stirring.
  7. Add the taro and tomatoes then simmer for 40 minutes or until pork is tender
  8. Put-in the sinigang mix and chili
  9. Add the string beans (and other vegetables if there are any) and simmer for 5 to 8 minutes
  10. Put-in the spinach, turn off the heat, and cover the pot. Let the spinach cook using the remaining heat in the pot.
  11. Serve hot. Share and enjoy!
